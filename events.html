2nd place for Hardware Robotics Design in the International Conference on Social Robotics,

Designed by Metodi Netzev, the open source robot head design won 2nd place in the hardware design competition part of the 10th International Conference on Social Robotics! It was valued for its adaptability for industry, hobbyists and capability to fit multiple sensors and equipment.

AI Hub Tampere

AI Hub Tampere is a new AI research center hosted by Tampere University and funded by public instruments. The center organizes workshops, helpdesk sessions, experimental piloting and other support for adopting AI in local companies. The formal opening ceremony was held on 10.12.2018 in Kampusareena and can be watched here

European Robotic Forum

The European Robotics Forum (ERF2018), the most influential meeting of the robotics community in Europe, will be held in Tampere, Finland, on 13-15 March 2018.

Over 800 European robotics top experts are expected to attend the conference. Under the theme "Robots and Us", ERF2018 covers current societal and technical themes related to the field of robotics.
